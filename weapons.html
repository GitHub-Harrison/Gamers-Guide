Weapons


Apex first released with a variation of weapons and have continued to release new weapons since the games launch, here are the weapons currently in the game:


      Assault rifles
    • HAVOC Rifle (Full-auto charged enerfy rifle)
    • VK-47 Flateline (Full-auto + semi-auto firing mode assault rifle)
    • Hemlok Burst AR (3-round burst assault rifle)
    • R-301 Carbine (Full-auto + semi-auto firing mode assault rifle)

      Sub machine guns
    • Alternator SMG (Twin barrel full-auto)
    • Prowler Burst PDW (5-round burst SMG)
    • R-99 SMG (Rapid-fire automatic SMG)
    • Volt SMG (Full-auto energy SMG)

      Light machine guns
    • Devotion LMG (Full-auto energy LMG)
    • M600 Spitfire (Full-auto LMG)
    • L-STAR EMG (Plasma-fueled heavy MG)

      Marksman weapons
    • G7 Scout (Semi-auto light marksman rifle)
    • 30-30 Repeater (Semi-auto heavy repeater)
    • Triple Take (Triple-barrel sniper rifle)
    • Bocek Compound Bow (Hold fire to increase power)

      Sniper rifles
    • Charge Rifle (Charged beam sniper rifle)
    • Longbow DMR (Semi-auto sniper rifle)
    • Kraber .50-Cal Sniper (Powerful bolt-action sniper rifle)
    • Sentinel (bolt-action sniper rifle)

      Shotguns
    • EVA-8 Auto (Full-auto shotgun)
    • Mastiff Shotgun (Powerful semi-auto shotgun)
    • Mozambique Shotgun (Triple-barrel shotgun pistol)
    • Peacekeeper (Lever-action shotgun)

      Pistols
    • RE-45 Auto (Full-auto pistol)
    • P2020 (Semi-auto pistol)
    • Wingman (High powered revolver)
